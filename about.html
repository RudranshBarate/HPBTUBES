About us

HPB Tube LLP, a sister concern of TPP Boilers Pvt. Ltd., is a trusted designer, fabricator, and erector of high-quality boiler components and equipment manufactured from steel plates, tubes, and pipes. We are a small-scale industry powered by the leadership of our highly experienced CEO, supported by a dedicated team of skilled professionals. Our leadership and workforce from a reputed boiler manufacturing organization with over 50 years of industry legacy, enabling us to deliver the technical excellence of a large enterprise while maintaining the personalized service, flexibility, and cost efficiency of a smaller organization. Our promoters and workforce collectively contribute over 500 man-years of experience in fabrication, tube bending, and welding of all types of tubes and pipes, including seamless, ERW, and rifle-bore varieties. We specialize in working with a wide range of materials such as carbon steels, alloy steels, and stainless steels, across all sizes, lengths, and specifications.
